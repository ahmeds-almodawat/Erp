# Finance Close Live Guide

Recommended sequence:

1. Import bank statement.
2. Match bank statement lines to journal lines.
3. Create bank reconciliation run.
4. Generate VAT settlement.
5. Review VAT settlement.
6. Confirm trial balance.
7. Confirm inventory reconciliation.
8. Confirm AP and AR reconciliation.
9. Confirm backup/restore.
10. Request period close.

Never close a period with unresolved critical findings.
