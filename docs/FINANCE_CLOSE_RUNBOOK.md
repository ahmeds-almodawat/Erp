# Finance Close Runbook

Before closing a period:

1. Confirm trial balance is balanced.
2. Confirm no critical unposted batches.
3. Reconcile inventory valuation to GL.
4. Reconcile AP subledger to GL.
5. Reconcile AR subledger to GL.
6. Reconcile bank accounts.
7. Review VAT summary.
8. Backup database.
9. Lock/close fiscal period only after checks pass.
